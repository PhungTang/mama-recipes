Chinese Steamed Egg

Description

This delicious and healthy dish is super easy to make

Ingredients

  • Eggs x 3
  • Spring onions x 1
  • Still water 200ml
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Beat eggs (x3) and pour in measuring jug
  2. Pour still water (200ml) into measuring jug and mix
  3. Add salt and black pepper
  4. Pour mixture into steaming dish
  5. Steam at medium temparture for 9 mins
  6. Add cooking oil and coat the top of the egg, this will help spread the soy sauce
  7. Finely chop spring onions and sprinkle on top
